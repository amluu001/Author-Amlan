The Author

Amlan
Debnath

Literary Historical Fiction · West Bengal, India

Born and rooted in West Bengal, Amlan Debnath writes literary historical fiction that breathes life into Bengal's layered past.

His prose weaves romance, mystery, and history into a tapestry of place and memory.

The Writing

Amlan's fiction is anchored in the Bhagirathi river and the Battle of Plassey (1757).

His debut trilogy, The Cartographer's Beloved, blends mystery with literary romance and the Bengali storytelling tradition.

Influences & Tradition

Amlan draws from the sharp observational intelligence of Feluda and the romantic sweep of classic Bengali fiction.

The work is as much about place as about people: Bengal as a living character, its rivers as witnesses.
